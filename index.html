01 — Bio

Stephen Peasley is a Calgary-based actor. He's been told he has a face made for software development—and for a long time, that's mostly what it did. Now he works in commercials, voice over, background film and television, and theatre—including a principal turn as Dan in Next to Normal at New West Theatre. His improv work includes performing with Dirty Laundry, creating and producing the weekly improvised soap opera Next Time, and co-producing The Kinkonauts' sold-out musical improv shows. Between bookings, he builds CueParrot, a script-rehearsal platform for actors.
