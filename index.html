01 — Bio

Stephen Peasley is a Calgary-based actor. He's been told he has a face made for software development—and for a long time, that's mostly what it did. Now he works in commercials, voice over, theatre, and background film and television. His improv work includes performing with Dirty Laundry, co-founding AAA Discount Improv, and co-producing The Kinkonauts' sold-out musical improv shows. Between bookings, he builds CueParrot, a script-rehearsal platform for actors. Open to film and television speaking-role auditions.
